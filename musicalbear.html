Musical (Lullaby) Bear

Excerpt of documentation


Video demonstration - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-KhFJ92v_HQ&t

Summary

Our product is an exciting plush toy bear, programmed to produce synthesised musical patterns depending on its physical orientation, or if it is being shaken. Each physical orientation of the bear reflects a particular activity that the bear is assumed to be partaking in with the child.
For instance - when the bear is laid on its back, it is assumed that it is sleeping with the child, and so would play a soothing soft lullaby. Depending on its orientation, the bear will play a synthesised musical pattern that reflects the mood of the child’s activity.
